About Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem

Our History

Founded in 1984 in Ankleshwar, Gujarat, Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar was established with a vision to serve as a center for Islamic learning and community welfare. Since its inception, it has grown into one of the most respected Islamic institutions in the region, offering free education and spiritual guidance to thousands of students. The masjid and its associated madrasa were built to preserve and promote the teachings of Islam, fostering an environment of knowledge, piety, and compassion.

Our Mission

At Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality Islamic education to all, regardless of financial or social barriers. We aim to nurture a generation of knowledgeable, ethical, and compassionate individuals who can contribute positively to society while upholding Islamic values. Our programs are designed to balance traditional Islamic scholarship with contemporary skills, ensuring our students are well-equipped for the modern world.

Educational Excellence

The masjid operates a comprehensive madrasa that offers courses in Hifz-e-Quran, Alim studies, Tafseer, and Arabic language, among others. With a dedicated team of experienced scholars and educators, we have successfully trained hundreds of students who have gone on to become scholars, qaris, and hafizs. In addition, we run a primary school recognized by the Gujarat government, providing secular education alongside Islamic studies to young learners.

Community Engagement

Beyond education, Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar is deeply committed to serving the Ankleshwar community. We organize regular religious gatherings, charity drives, and social welfare programs to support the underprivileged. We also facilitate community events such as Jumma prayers, Islamic seminars, and naat competitions, fostering a sense of unity and spiritual connection among residents.

Our Legacy

Over the past four decades, Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar has made significant contributions to Islamic education and community development in Ankleshwar. We take pride in our alumni, who serve as imams, educators, and community leaders across India and beyond. With the support of our donors and the dedication of our staff, we continue to expand our initiatives, striving to make a lasting impact on society.

About the Committee

The Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar is managed by a dedicated committee consisting of the following esteemed members: Haji Iftikhar Sahab, Haji Irfan Sahab, and Hafiz Abdul Aleem Sahab. Their leadership ensures the masjid continues to thrive as a center of learning and community service.

We invite you to visit Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar, learn more about our work, and join us in our mission to spread knowledge and compassion. Your support and prayers are invaluable to our continued success.

Admission

Maktab Admission Rules

  • Eligibility: Applicants must be approximately 5 years old with a willingness to learn Nurani Qaida, Hifz-e-Quran, Urdu, and writing skills.
  • Dress Code: Students must wear traditional Islamic attire (kurta-pajama, topi) during classes and prayers.
  • Attendance: 100% attendance is mandatory for all classes and daily prayers unless excused by the administration.
  • Mobile Phones: Use of mobile phones is prohibited during classes and study hours to maintain focus.
  • Conduct: Students must adhere to Islamic etiquette, respect teachers, and maintain discipline at all times.
  • Residence: Residential students must follow hostel rules, including fixed meal and sleep schedules.

Our Maktab

Maktab Curriculum

Our Maktab is designed for young children, starting at age 5, to learn the basics of Islamic education. The curriculum begins with Nurani Qaida, progresses to Hifz-e-Quran, and includes Urdu reading, writing, and basic Islamic teachings.

Our Teachers

The Maktab is led by two dedicated teachers: Moulana Jarrar Sahab, Imam of Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar, and Moulana Mohsin Sahab, former Muazzin of Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar.

Donate

Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar’s various charitable activities are financed by contributions given by generous people.

Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar accepts all sorts of contributions that are appropriate to its mission such as donations, Zakat (Islamic alms), Waqf, Sadaqa, grants, gifts, and bequests made for its favor.

Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar also accepts designated contributions for specific purposes such as: sponsoring orphans or a specific project such as building mosques, health clinic, drilling a well, and the like.

The money given to us in charity is used for the specific purpose in case the donor specifies the purpose such as building a mosque and Madrasah or digging the borehole or feeding the children etc. or else it will be used wherever it is most needed for the cause of Masjid e Aaisha Qadeem Hifazat Nagar if purpose not specified.
